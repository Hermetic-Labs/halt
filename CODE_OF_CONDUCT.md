# Code of Conduct

## Our Mission

HALT exists to serve medics working in conflict zones, disaster areas, and resource-limited environments. We hold our community to the same standard of care expected in the field.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on what's best for the people this software serves
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Prioritize clinical accuracy over personal preference
- Respect the urgency of this work

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Deliberately introducing inaccurate medical data
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a clinical setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@hermeticlabs.app. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
